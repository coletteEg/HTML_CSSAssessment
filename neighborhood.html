Springville Utah, the art city

Sprinville is a town in Utah County nestled inbetween Provo Peak and Spanish Fork Peak. Springville is known as the Art City because it houses the oldest musuem of visual fine arts in Utah- it opened in 1937! Springville has a population of less than 50,000 and is considered a bedroom community for those that work in the Provo-Orem and Salt Lake.

Gingers Cafe is a great place to try something new!

You MUST check out the Springville Museum of Art during your stay

Another classic place to eat here is La Casita- a tasty Mexican Restaurant
